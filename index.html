Community Workshops

Free workshops for personal and professional growth in the Irving, TX community.

Who We Are

We are an organization serving the local community by providing FREE WORKSHOPs about topics critical to life in the US. If you are an international family, college student, early career, local job seeker, or anyone looking to grow themselves, these workshops are for you!

What We Offer

We are hosting workshops on topics related to Professional Development, Strengthening Family-life, Personal Growth, Financial Success, & Healthy Living. The purpose of the workshop is to guide and strengthen your life in these areas and improve our local community. These workshops are free of cost to the attendees however your registration is required to attend each individual session.
